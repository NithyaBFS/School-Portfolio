History



Holy Cross GHSS ,Teppakulam , Trichy-2 is a well reputed girl's school. Holy Cross Girls’ Higher Secondary School, run by the sisters of the cross, has been in the forefront of women’s education for eight decades and more. The school was established in the year 1902 and it has crossed more than 114 years of education excellence. Following the state board syllabus, the school dedicates itself to build responsible citizens for the society.
This School was founded in 1902 as a primary school and in 1905, the school was upgraded to High school. Since 1978 , it has been functioning as a Higher Secondary School with a permanent recongnition.

Vision & Mission

VISION

Inspired by the love of our Lord Jesus Christ, we intend to form well integrated persons who will strive to transform the society by meeting the challenges of life and taking a stand in struggles of the poor and the marginalized.


MISSION

Drawing inspiration from the lives of Jesus and our foundress Mother Claudine Echernier, we strive to reveal to all, the merciful love of the father and the liberating power of the Paschal Mystery through the ministry of Education.

Alumni Association

Welcome to Holy Cross Girls’ Higher Secondary School alumni network. The Alumni association is formed to foster and strengthen a lifelong commitment between the school and its students by providing for them to engage in spiritual, professional, social and community service activities. The association is open to any person who has graduated from our school. On this website, you can be an active and engaged member of the community, stay in touch and reconnect with our classmates, and keep up to date about alumni events. Our school has planned special events each year at our school premises for the reunion of the alumnae. We invite you to explore and participate in these activities to build and maintain positive working relationship with the school and your friends. By giving your time and expertise, we will move forward with enthusiasm and fortitude striving to give the best opportunities to our school community.
